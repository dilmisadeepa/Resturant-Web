Our Story

Rooted in Tradition, Refined for Today

Founded in 2010, Maison Ceylan was born from a passion to honour Sri Lanka's rich culinary heritage. Every dish on our menu carries a memory, a family recipe, a spice route story. Our head chef trained in Paris but returned home to cook with his grandmother's recipes — and that is the soul of everything we serve.
